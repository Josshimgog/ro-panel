Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 7, 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how the Staff Administrative Platform (SAP), operated by TEnuN78, collects, stores, and uses your information when you log in and use the platform. This policy applies to all users of the SAP platform who authenticate using Roblox OAuth and Firebase.

2. Information We Collect

When you use SAP, we may collect the following data:

  • Roblox username, ID, avatar, and current rank in any whitelisted groups
  • Your roles assigned within SAP (Support, Trainer, Manager, etc.)
  • Group-related activity such as quota completion, ticket logs, and training logs
  • Uploaded ticket transcripts (.html, .csv files)
  • In-game activity time (if applicable to quotas)

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:

  • Authenticate you through Roblox OAuth
  • Verify whether you are a member of approved groups
  • Display your staff profile, rank, and assigned quotas
  • Track and review activity (quotas, trainings, ticket logs)
  • Enable workspace-specific features based on your permissions

4. Role-Based Access

Only users assigned certain roles or ranks can view or modify specific parts of the system. Access to sensitive actions like editing quotas, uploading ticket logs, or changing tab settings is restricted by role and configured within Admin Hub by the owner.

5. File Uploads

Ticket transcripts and training logs can be uploaded as files. These files are securely stored and visible only to those with authorized access in the workspace. Files must be in .html or .csv format.

6. Cookies & Session Data

We may use browser storage or session cookies to maintain your authentication status during your login session. No tracking or advertising cookies are used. These cookies are temporary and cleared when your session ends.

7. Third-Party Services

We use third-party services such as:

  • Firebase (for authentication and limited data storage)
  • Roblox API (for validating user information and group rank)
  • Vercel or Replit (for hosting the platform)

These providers have their own privacy practices. We do not control their use of data beyond what's necessary for functionality.

8. Data Retention

We retain user logs and data for up to 6 months or until a workspace administrator deletes it. If you leave a workspace, your logs may still be visible to authorized users for a limited time unless deletion is requested.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your Roblox-based staff profile within SAP
  • Request correction of any inaccuracies in your data
  • Request deletion of your quota, logs, or uploaded files (via a workspace admin)
  • Withdraw access by leaving the Roblox group

10. Data Security

All data is secured using industry-standard protections. Firebase Authentication, HTTPS encryption, and role-based access controls ensure that only authorized users can access sensitive data.

11. Children's Privacy

SAP is designed for Roblox users. While Roblox permits users under 13, we do not knowingly collect private data other than what’s available via Roblox OAuth. No email addresses, passwords, or personal contact information are collected from users.

12. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated at any time. The effective date at the top will reflect the latest version. Continued use of SAP after changes implies your acceptance.
